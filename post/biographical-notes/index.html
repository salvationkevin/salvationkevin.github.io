Biographical notes

2022-04-05 3 min read

BACKGROUD

Kevin has 14+ years experience in consumer and market research field. Previously he worked at Synovate, Ipsos and GfK. Now he is an independent researcher and consultant specialized in consumer and market behavior, brand strategy, user experience, and product & service innovation.

Apart from his continuing exploration in business field, Kevin also proactively engages himself with consulting practices in philanthropy and non-profit field. Since early 2017, he has been working as a volunteer in a social enterprise to offer professional advisory services for NPOs, corporate CSR, government and individuals as well.

SPECIALTY

Kevin has continued to strengthen his capability in qualitative research. His specialty includes various type of qualitative research methods, such as focus group, in-depth interview, ethnography, anthropological observation diary, online techniques (i.e. focus group, OBB). Currently he also adapts to employ hybrid methodologies to work out solution for clients.
